What is Collab?

Collab was designed due to the increased difficulty
of school because of the COVID-19 epidemic. In past times
study sessions, reviews, and meeting with friends for cramming
was an easy task. With the current global pandemic meeting in
person has become a much more deadly prospect. In order to
adapt with the times Collab was designed in order to
allow students to connect with their friends and classmates.

Ratings and Rewards

Collab was designed with the intention of helping students find ways to study with each other. In order to promote this we have a rating system for people that allows them to provide feeback on the quality time they spent with tutors and fellow students. Those who acheive high ratings will receive extra benefits such as private call rooms, unique profile borders, and much more.

How it Works

You start on the sign-up page where you need to create an account. From there the system will redirect you to the home page where your study experince begins!

Home Page

The Home Page has a Navigation bar on top in order to provide quick and easy access to the various avenues for help. Alternativly, you can also use the "Get Started" button.

Courses

The courses page has a comprehensive Calendar showing all the avalible study times for the current month. The Calendar will show study times on a subject by subject basis. Clicking on the Courses in the lower tabs take you to individually customized pages for each subject.

Subjects

Each subject has its own page with numerous features included. Chat with other students using the live chat box, or help someone out in the forum posts. If you're needing some extra assistance then hop into a call with someone!
